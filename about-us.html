About Us

WELCOME TO A1 ENGINEERING COMPANY LIMITED


A1 Engineering Company Limited is a multi-faceted company specializing in project management, power projects, renewable energy services, procurement solutions, and serving as importers' representatives and primary distributors of electrical and power products. Our reputation is built on the sale of authentic electrical and power materials from our renowned electrical shop/warehouse.

Our team is composed of seasoned professionals with substantial experience in power solutions, procurement, project management, marketing, risk assessment, and various other services. Collaborating with key players in the power sector and construction industry, we endeavor to bridge gaps along the value chain, optimizing costs while ensuring a consistent power supply for end-users and reducing business overhead expenses.

Our primary goal is to provide reliable and efficient energy services, unwavering in our commitment to quality and safety. We aim not only to showcase our skills and competitive pricing but also to establish enduring client relationships that extend well beyond the completion of our initial projects.

With a collective experience spanning over ninety years in both the public and private sectors, our team is well-equipped to deliver efficient services from project initiation to completion. At Hikima, our focus is on providing clients with practical, bespoke solutions that align with their strategic objectives.
